Dreamland is a country-wide system of representative areas of Argentina significance. Dreamland consist five districts included the history district, the magic district, the future district, the galaxy district, and the district of hope. They are protected for public understanding, appreciation and enjoyment, while being maintained in an unimpaired state for future generations. Dreamland have existed in Argentina for well over a century. Read more....
