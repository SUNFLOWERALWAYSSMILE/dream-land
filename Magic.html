Magic District is a non profit organization which hosts an annual 60-day summer fair, seasonal amusement park, and arena . It usually begins in July, and ends in September. Other than the fair, Magic District is also the home of amusement park, a horse racetrack (which also bears the name Hastings Park), the Pacific Coliseum (an ice hockey arena), and Agrodome. It was the site of Empire Stadium, which was demolished and replaced with a public soccer field. It is also the location of Skatepark, a skateboard bowl located to the south of the Pacific Coliseum.
